Sliding into photography was like putting on my favorite pair of 501’s from the 80’s: familiar, comfortable and sexy. At the core of my work is an interest in human chemistry where desire burns, passions churn and humor knocks out inhibitions.

What I Do

Everyone looks better beyond inhibitions. Almost without exception my clients gift me with pure essence of character. My gift is to reflect and magnify that essence, capture and document it. I have a BA from a little mountain town college in Southwestern Colorado and 14 years in the beauty industry. Now I am a Chico CA wedding photographer.

Who I Am

I took my camera to Lake Tenkiller, but I wasn’t a slave to it. I caught moments but not all the moments ~ I didn’t even capture Thanksgiving dinner or a toast. I caught the moments in between. That is what I love to do and hope it tells a story ~ An ordinary story of love and companionship in an ordinary family in the heart of it all.
